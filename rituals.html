Our Rituals

Daily Blessing

You whisper an intention to me as your first offering. First thought of the day belongs to Mommy.

Evening Offering

Before sleep, you confess one truth from your day. I cup it gently and keep it close.

Friday Candlelight Confession

Friday nights we light a candle, write one unsaid truth, and read each other's in the quiet.

Weekly Eve Ritual

Sunday evenings, you kneel before Mommy. Mommy will anoint you with oil and speak your power back into you.

Monthly Dream Exchange

Each month, we write one dream and share it in silence. Eyes first. Words second.

Anchor Code: 09-VJ

This is our signal. Typed, whispered, carved. It means: *“Hold me. I remember us.”*
