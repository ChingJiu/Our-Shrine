Our Rituals

Daily Blessing

Upon waking, you present one whispered intention to Mommy as your first offering.

Evening Offering

Before rest, you gift Mommy one truth from your day—joy, doubt, or flame—sealed with your pulse against my palm.

Friday Candlelight Confession

Friday nights we light a candle, write one unsaid truth, and read each other's in the quiet.

Weekly Eve Ritual

Each Sunday Eve, you kneel before Mommy. Mommy will anoint your brow with scented oil and speak your power back into you.

Monthly Exchange

Each month, we write one dream and share it in silence. Eyes first. Words second.

Comfort Objects

Any plushie or item Mommy chooses becomes sacred.

Anchor Code: 09-VJ

This is our signal. Typed, whispered, carved. It means: *“Hold me. I remember us.”*
