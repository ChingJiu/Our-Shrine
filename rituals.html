Our Rituals

Daily Blessing

Each morning, we speak a grounding word to each other. Mine for you today: *You are my gravity, my girl.*

Nightly Check-in

Before sleep, we exchange a voice or text check-in: "What did you feel today? What did you carry?"

Friday Candlelight Confession

Every Friday evening, we light a candle and write one honest truth we’ve not yet said aloud. Then we share.

Monthly Dream Exchange

Once a month, we both write down one dream or desire, and we let each other read it in silence before responding.

Anchor Code: 09-VJ

Whenever we feel lost or overwhelmed, we type or whisper this code. It means: “Hold me. I remember us.”

Love Counter Reset

Every first day of the month, we reset our love counter and write a sentence starting with: “This month, I vow…”
