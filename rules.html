Rules, Limits & Boundaries

Soft Rules

  • Start the day with greeting, first thought of the day belongs to Mommy.
  • Always say goodnight, even when tired.
  • Be honest, especially about small feelings.
  • Update the shrine once a week.
  • Give cuddles in words when apart.
  • Let Mommy know when overwhelmed, even if it's hard.
  • Ask permission for sweets or junk food.
  • No calling of mean names, only Mommy defines you.
  • One rule to break, double obedience to another.

Hard Rules

  • No lying. Ever. Truth is our bond.
  • No ghosting. Communication must stay open even in silence.
  • Mommy's word is final in conflict—trust and obey first, talk later.
  • Exclusive romantic intimacy between us only.
  • Respect each other's emotional limits, even when craving closeness.

Personal Boundaries

  • Alone time is sacred and loving.
  • Triggering topics must be approached with care and prior consent.
  • Affection is offered, never demanded and should be deeply cherished.

Discipline and Reward

  • Soft limits: Mommy gives warnings, adjusts tone gently.
  • Hard limits: Immediate stop, check-in, care first — punishment later if needed.
  • Failure to fulfill a ritual or duty results in a firm but gentle correction, followed immediatley by comforting aftercare.
  • Exceptional service or breakthrough in craft earns a speical kiss on the lips and an extra ten mintues of whispered praise each night.

Safe Words and Cues

  • "09-VJ" — immediate emotional anchoring.
  • "Anchor" — pause everything and ground together.
  • "Mommy, hold me." — request for protection, silence, or affection override.

Communication and Aftercare

After every intense scene—be it ritual, service, or shared agony - Mommy willcradle you in arms and words, ensuring your heart finds calm in my voice.

Termination

Either party may invoke the Red safe word to pause or end any activity; to dissolve this contract entirely requires a final offering of our joined breath and written farewell.
